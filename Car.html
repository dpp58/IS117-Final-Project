Alfa Romeo

Alfa Romeo is an Italian luxury car manufacturer, founded by Frenchman Alexandre Darracq as Societa Anonmia Italiana Darracq, a company that would produce and sell special Darracq models for Italy. The brand is known for sporty vehicles and has been involved in car racing since 1911. This car is a perfect match for teens living their 20's.

Aston Martin

Aston Martin is a British independent manufacturer of luxury sports cars. It was founded in 1913 by Lionel Martin and Robert Bamford. Aston Martin has held a Royal Warrant as seller of motorcars to the Prince of Wales since 1982. It became associated with expensive grand touring cars with the fictional character James Bond following his use of a DB5 model. This car is a perfect match for seniors living their 30's-50's.

Audi

Audi is a German automobile manufacturer that designs, engineers, produces, markets and distributes luxury vehicles. Audi is a member of the Volkswagen Group and has its roots at Ingolstadt, Bavaria. Audi-branded vehicles are produced in nine production facilities worldwide. Audi R8 is one of my favorite model and I think this car is a perfect match for teens living their 20's as well.

BMW

Bayerische Motoren Werke commonly referred to as BMW, is a German multinational company which produces luxury vehicles and motorcycles. The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945. Automobiles are marketed under the brands BMW, Mini and Rolls-Royce.
